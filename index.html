Know about this website

CollegeKart is an online marketplace made specially for college students. Students can buy and sell useful items like books, calculators, lab equipment, hostel essentials, electronics, and other college products at affordable prices.

Our goal is to help students save money and make it easier to reuse items instead of buying everything new. CollegeKart creates a simple and student-friendly platform for connecting buyers and sellers within college communities.
